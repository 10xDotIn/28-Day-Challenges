# Customer Lifetime Value Report

## Executive Summary
8,000 customers. $15.6M in predicted 3-year value.
But 73.4% of that comes from just 10% of customers.
Average CLV = $1,955. Churn rate = 44.1%.

## Customer Archetype Breakdown
- **Future VIP**: 642 customers (8.0%) → $10.5M CLV (67.2%)
- **Loyal Regular**: 1,492 customers (18.6%) → $4.0M CLV (25.6%)
- **Promising New**: 1,149 customers (14.4%) → $0.6M CLV (4.0%)
- **Discount Addict**: 1,555 customers (19.4%) → $0.4M CLV (2.8%)
- **Fading Away**: 1,111 customers (13.9%) → $0.1M CLV (0.4%)
- **One-and-Done**: 2,051 customers (25.6%) → $0.0M CLV (0.0%)

## First Purchase DNA
VIPs start with higher first-order values and lower discounts.
Heavy discounters attract Discount Addicts — not future VIPs.

## 30-Day Signals That Matter
Top predictor: **First Order Value** (importance: 0.284)
Model R² = 0.508 — these early signals explain most CLV variation.

## Channel Quality Ranking
Best channel: **referral** (highest avg CLV)
Worst channel: **paid_ads** (lowest avg CLV)

## The Discount Problem
Customers acquired at 0-5% discount have $3765 avg CLV.
Customers acquired at 20%+ discount have $181 avg CLV.
That's a 95.2% drop in lifetime value from heavy discounting.

## Churn Early Warning
Churn rate overall: 44.1%
Fading Away customers show high days_since_last_order and low email engagement.

## CLV Tier Breakdown
- **Platinum**: 800 customers → $11.5M total CLV
- **Gold**: 1,600 customers → $3.5M total CLV
- **Silver**: 2,400 customers → $0.7M total CLV
- **Bronze**: 3,200 customers → $0.0M total CLV

## Strategic Recommendations
- **INVEST** → Future VIPs & Loyal Regulars: increase retention spend
- **NURTURE** → Promising New: onboarding sequences, early engagement
- **CONVERT** → Discount Addicts: gradual discount reduction program
- **LET GO** → One-and-Done: stop wasting retention budget
- **SAVE** → Fading Away: re-engagement campaigns before churn

## 5 Actions to Increase Total CLV
1. Cap first-order discounts at 10% — saves significant CLV per acquired customer
2. Double down on referral acquisition — best quality customers
3. Trigger second-order campaign within 14 days of first purchase
4. Build early-warning alert for Fading Away customers (days_since_last_order > 60)
5. Create VIP fast-track: customers with 30d sessions > median get white-glove treatment

## Final Verdict
- **Most valuable archetype**: Future VIP
- **Worst acquisition channel**: paid_ads
- **Strongest CLV predictor**: First Order Value
- **Biggest immediate win**: Reduce first-order discounts above 15% — instant CLV uplift
