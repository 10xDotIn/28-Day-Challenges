Recommended Actions — Priority Order
01

Deep-Clean Audit

Cleanliness is the #1 complaint. Deploy post-checkout inspection checklists at all properties immediately.

02

Staff Service Training

Rude staff and poor reception attitude appear in the top negative phrases. Mandatory hospitality retraining required.

03

Fix the Rating Gap

182 guests gave 5 stars but wrote negative reviews. Redesign the post-stay survey to capture true sentiment.

04

Upgrade WiFi

167 complaints cite slow internet. For business travelers this is non-negotiable. Upgrade bandwidth by Q2.

05

Noise Mitigation

202 noise complaints involve thin walls and street noise. Proactively offer quieter upper-floor rooms at check-in.

06

Amplify What Works

Location and breakfast drive the most praise. Top-rated hotels hit 90%+ positivity — replicate their playbook.
