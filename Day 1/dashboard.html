1 Regional Sales Performance

West dominates with 30% of total revenue. Midwest lags at 15%, a $134M gap from the top.

Actionable Outcomes

  • Double down on West region — allocate 30% of marketing budget to sustain leadership; pilot premium product launches here first
  • Investigate Midwest underperformance — conduct retailer-level audit; explore partnerships with regional chains
  • Rebalance South inventory — highest units (492K) but lower revenue suggests price sensitivity; test bundle offers

2 Product Category Profitability

Men's Street Footwear leads in both revenue ($209M) and margin (39.6%). Women's Athletic Footwear has growth potential.

Actionable Outcomes

  • Expand Men's Street Footwear line — highest margin (39.6%) and revenue; increase SKU variety and limited editions
  • Boost Women's Athletic Footwear — lowest revenue ($107M) but solid 36.6% margin; invest in targeted campaigns
  • Review Men's Athletic pricing — lowest margin (33.7%) despite $154M revenue; optimize cost structure or adjust pricing

3 Sales Channel Effectiveness

4 Retailer Performance Comparison

West Gear & Foot Locker drive 51% of revenue. Sports Direct has the best margin (40.7%) with half the volume.

Actionable Outcomes

  • Strengthen Sports Direct partnership — best margin (40.7%); negotiate expanded shelf space and co-branded campaigns
  • Grow Amazon & Walmart channels — combined only 17% of revenue; huge addressable market untapped
  • Renegotiate Kohl's terms — low volume and average margins; evaluate ROI of promotional spend

5 Revenue Growth Trajectory

2021 saw explosive growth — July 2021 peaked at $78.3M (4.8x July 2020). Strong seasonality with Q2-Q3 peaks.

Actionable Outcomes

  • Pre-stock for summer surge — May-Aug consistently peak; ensure inventory ramp-up begins in March
  • Capitalize on Dec holiday spike — Dec 2021 hit $77.8M; create holiday bundles and gift campaigns
  • Address Q4 2020 dip pattern — monitor for seasonal slowdowns; deploy targeted promotions in Oct-Nov
