Seasonal Weather Playbook

Strategy Board

🔴 PREPARE

  • Staff up delivery teams on forecast rain days (+$2,389/day)
  • Reduce outdoor event staffing on storm days
  • Pre-position e-commerce inventory before storms
  • Weather insurance for outdoor events ($4,078/day at risk)

🟢 CAPITALIZE

  • Rain-triggered ads for delivery & ride-hailing
  • Flash sales during storms for e-commerce
  • Rainy-day specials at movie theaters
  • Cold-weather warm drink promotions at coffee shops

🟡 HEDGE

  • Restaurants: shift to delivery model on rain days
  • Retail: boost online + free shipping in bad weather
  • Outdoor events: indoor backup venues
  • Cross-partner rain-winners with rain-losers

⚪ IGNORE

  • Cloudy weather — minimal revenue impact
  • Light wind — noise, not signal
  • Fog — affects very few businesses
  • Humidity alone (without heat) — negligible effect
