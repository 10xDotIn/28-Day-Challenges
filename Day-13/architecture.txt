🎯 Business Problem

Banks run marketing campaigns to sell products.

But:

Contacting customers costs money
Most customers do NOT respond
Some customers are more likely to respond
Repeated contact can reduce effectiveness

👉 So the problem is:

“How do we find whom to target and where we are wasting effort?”

📊 What We Are Doing Today

We are NOT building any model.

We are doing analysis to answer 3 things:

Are we over-contacting customers?
Which customer groups actually respond?
Does past campaign behavior matter?
🧠 What Students Should Understand
More effort ≠ better results
Not all customers are equal
Some customers are waste
Some customers are opportunity
Decisions > predictions


🤖 5 Example Prompts (Agentic Analysis)
Prompt 1

Analyze this dataset and identify where the campaign is wasting effort. Focus on repeated contacts and low-response patterns.

Prompt 2

Find which customer segments have the highest response rate and which have the lowest. Suggest how targeting should change.

Prompt 3

Check if increasing the number of contacts improves conversion or creates diminishing returns. Explain in business terms.

Prompt 4

Analyze the impact of previous campaign outcomes (poutcome). Should past success influence future targeting?

Prompt 5

Give 3 clear business rules the bank should follow to improve campaign performance based on this dataset.
