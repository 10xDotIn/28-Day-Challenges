⚠️ What this dataset can and cannot tell us

Available in the data: Username, self-reported city/province, review dates, hotels reviewed, ratings pattern, review text.
NOT available: Email address, phone number, real name, IP address, account ID, payment info.

To get contact information, the review platform itself (TripAdvisor, Booking.com, etc.) would need to be involved — they hold account registration data. Law enforcement can subpoena that data if fraud is confirmed. What we can do: build a strong evidence dossier for each account so the platform or authorities have grounds to act.
