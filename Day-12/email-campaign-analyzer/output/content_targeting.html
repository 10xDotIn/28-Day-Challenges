The Content-Customer Mismatch

Out of 35 segment-campaign combinations, only 5 actually increase purchases. 24 combinations actively REDUCE purchase rates. The same campaign can be a winner for one segment and a disaster for another.

For example, Summer Sale has 0 positive combos but destroys purchases for VIP (-18.6pp) and Regular (-16.4pp). Meanwhile Flash Deal works great for Inactive (+5.7pp) but hurts Frequent Buyers (-7.2pp). Content targeting matters more than volume.

Bottom Line: Target the Content, Not Just the Volume

1. Inactive users respond to Flash Deal (+5.7pp) and Win-Back (+2.8pp) but are HURT by Welcome Series (-3.8pp).

2. New Users respond to Weekend Special (+6.4pp) and Flash Deal (+4.1pp) but are HURT by Loyalty Reward (-8.7pp) and New Arrival (-6.6pp).

3. VIPs only respond to Welcome Series (+4.8pp). Every other campaign REDUCES their purchases, especially Summer Sale (-18.6pp).

4. Frequent Buyers only respond to New Arrival (+3.5pp). Sales campaigns kill their purchase rate.

5. Regular users barely respond to any email. Best bet is Welcome Series (+0.9pp) but it's marginal.

The fix isn't "email more" or "email less" — it's "email the RIGHT content to the RIGHT person."
