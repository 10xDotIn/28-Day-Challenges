Action Playbook

1
Immediately stop emailing VIP, Regular, and Frequent Buyer segments.

2
Stop emailing New Users.
-2.5pp lift means emails are counterproductive. These users need onboarding, not sales emails. Save $59.
3
Focus all email resources on Inactive users.
Only segment with positive lift (+1.1pp). Email all 400 non-emailed Inactive users.
4
Investigate why email hurts purchase rates.
Negative lift across 4 segments suggests emails may be annoying customers, creating decision fatigue, or diluting urgency. Run A/B tests with reduced frequency.
5
Reallocate the $268 saved to improve email quality.
Better subject lines (54.5% open drop), better content (60.2% click drop), and targeted offers for Inactive users where email actually drives incremental revenue.
